Abora Links

Considering the importance of Xanadu, I am surprised by the lack of detailed information on implementing Xanadu like systems. Hopefully the following URLs will lead you to some of the central nodes for Xanalogical knowledge and activity. Beyond the Xanadu sites themselves, Sunless-Sea by Jeff Rush deserves a visit.

If you come across any other useful links, don't hesitate to contact me -- David Jones
